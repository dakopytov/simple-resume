# Royal Park Day Care & Day Surgery Centre

**Location:** Baku, Azerbaijan  
**Project Type:** Healthcare / Day Care and Day Surgery Centre

## Project Description

Royal Park Day Care & Day Surgery Centre was developed as a healthcare project focused on creating a more human-centered and welcoming patient environment. The design intent was to move away from the typical sterile, white-walled hospital atmosphere and instead create a space that responds to the individual needs of patients and users.

The project involved two existing buildings separated by a street. The main building accommodated the primary clinical functions, while the smaller building provided technical and maintenance support services. The overall approach aimed to make the best possible use of the existing construction and site conditions, including the lower garden and the surrounding views, to create a more comfortable and context-sensitive healthcare experience.
